# 3HD2K: Premium Cinematic Experience — Project Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Project Overview
3HD2K is an AI-native, high-fidelity movie ticketing and social platform designed to provide a premium, immersive "Cinematic Noir" experience. The platform bridges the gap between digital booking and physical theater attendance through unique social features and interactive pre/post-show experiences.

**Brand Identity:**
*   **Aesthetic:** Modern Editorial / Cinematic Noir.
*   **Primary Color:** #E50914 (3HD2K Red).
*   **Typography:** Times New Roman (Editorial feel) & Bebas Neue (Logo/Headings).
*   **Theme:** Dark Mode, high contrast, glassmorphism accents.

---

## 2. Target Audience
*   **Cinephiles:** Users looking for high-quality, specialized formats (IMAX, 3D).
*   **Social Viewers:** Solo movie-goers looking to meet like-minded fans.
*   **Groups:** Friends and families seeking seamless booking and payment coordination.

---

## 3. Core Functional Requirements

### 3.1. Account & Identity
*   **Authentication:** Secure login/registration, OTP verification for transactions, and password recovery.
*   **Member Profile:** Dashboard for personal info, transaction history, and loyalty tier status.
*   **3HD2K Rewards:** Loyalty points system for redeeming e-vouchers (popcorn, tickets, merchandise).

### 3.2. Discovery & Selection
*   **Dynamic Homepage:** Featured "Now Showing" carousel, category filters (Action, Sci-Fi, etc.), and "Coming Soon" sections.
*   **Advanced Search:** Search by movie title, director, or cast; filter by format (IMAX, 2D, 3D) and cinema location.
*   **Cinema Locator:** Integrated map view to find the nearest 3HD2K theaters with distance tracking.
*   **Movie Detail Pages:** High-fidelity hero sections, trailer integration, cast/crew bios, and user reviews.

### 3.3. Premium Booking Flow
*   **Unified Booking:** Streamlined flow: Cinema Selection → Showtime Selection → Real-time Seating Map.
*   **Concessions (Bắp Nước):** Pre-purchase popcorn and soda combos during the booking flow to skip lines.
*   **Split & Lock (Group Booking):** Allows a leader to lock a block of seats for 15 minutes while members pay for their own tickets individually.
*   **Digital Ticketing:** Generation of secure QR code tickets with real-time screening details.

---

## 4. Unique Experience Features (Differentiators)

### 4.1. Cine-Match (Social Zone)
*   Anonymous pairing based on movie taste.
*   Dedicated "Social Zone" seating areas in theaters.
*   Identity reveal only via QR scan at the theater entrance.

### 4.2. Cine-Bet Minigame
*   Pre-show interaction (15-20 mins before start).
*   Prediction-based betting on movie outcomes or Rotten Tomatoes scores.
*   Rewards: Instant e-vouchers for concessions.

### 4.3. After-Credit Lounge
*   Exclusive virtual chat/audio rooms unlocked post-screening.
*   Verified access restricted to attendees of that specific screening.
*   15-minute discussion window to prevent spoilers and optimize resources.

---

## 5. Technical Requirements
*   **Responsive Design:** Full parity between Desktop (Immersive) and Mobile (Utility) experiences.
*   **Real-time Sync:** Live seating updates and group booking timers.
*   **Payment Integration:** Support for e-wallets (MoMo, ZaloPay) and international cards.
*   **Localization:** Entire interface localized in Vietnamese, with VND currency support.
